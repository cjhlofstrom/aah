Inspiring and empowering innovators

to help solve humanity's biggest challenges.

above all human is a conference examining the intersection of innovation and technology, with a focus on its implications for culture and society, and especially what it means for us as individuals. Now more than ever, humans have the opportunity to shape and implement the future as the speed of technological innovation is becoming exponentially faster in our lifetimes. We as humans have 'upgraded', transforming from consumers into producers, from bystanders into participants, from citizens into world-changers.

What will we decide to be the things that matter? What will we seek to change? Will we use these new 'superpowers' for good or evil? We have the opportunity to choose to be instruments of change or get carried along in its wake. In order to shape the future, we need to better understand ourselves and the edges of what's currently probable.

above all human is for founders, makers, and innovators who want to be better prepared to anticipate the future, embrace opportunities, build innovative products, and be more effective entrepreneurs.

Speakers

Justin Kan

YC partner, cofounder of Twitch.tv and Justin.tv

Justin Kan

Justin Kan popularised “lifecasting” when he broadcast his life 24/7 to the web in 2007. He’s the co-founder of both Justin.tv, and Twitch.tv, which was recently bought by Amazon. He’s also a part-time partner at Y Combinator.

@justinkan

Tracy Chou

Senior Engineer at Pinterest, formerly Quora

Tracy Chou

Tracy Chou is the woman who single-handedly pushed large tech giants to disclose their gender diversity statistics. She’s a software engineer at Pinterest, previously at Quora, where she works to better the culture for women and minorities.

@triketora

Joyce Kim

MIT, Founding member
of Stellar.org

Joyce Kim

Joyce Kim is a MIT Media Lab director’s fellow and executive director at Stellar, which focuses on using cryptocurrency to allow people to economically empower themselves. She believes that financial access is a prerequisite to fulfill all human rights.

@joyce

Morten Lund

Leading European entrepreuner, early Skype investor

Morten Lund

Morten Lund is one of Europe’s best known and boldest investors and entrepreneurs, who made his mark as one of the first investors in Skype. He’s currently involved in CodersTrust, which helps workers in Bangladesh upgrade their skills to make more money on freelance marketplaces.

@ML

Cameron Adams

Cofounder of Canva, formerly UI designer at Google

Cameron Adams

Cameron Adams is a co-founder at Canva and their leading web design craftsperson. He previously was a lead UI designer at Google, where he worked on Google Wave. He has authored four books on web design and regularly speaks at conferences around the world.

@themaninblue
